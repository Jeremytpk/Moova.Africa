About Moova

Building bridges between the African diaspora and home, one package at a time.

Our Story

Moova was born from a simple observation: millions of members of the African diaspora living in the USA want to send packages, gifts, and essential items to their families back home. Traditional shipping options are expensive, slow, and often unreliable.

At the same time, thousands of travelers fly between the USA and Africa every day with unused luggage space. We saw an opportunity to connect these two groups in a way that benefits everyone.

Moova is more than just a logistics platform - it's a community built on trust, affordability, and the shared experience of staying connected to home.

Our Mission

To provide the African diaspora with an affordable, reliable, and community-driven alternative to traditional shipping services, while enabling travelers to earn money from their spare luggage space.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted peer-to-peer logistics network connecting the global African diaspora with their home countries, making it easy and affordable to send love across continents.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Community

Built by and for the African diaspora. We understand the importance of staying connected to home.

Trust

Verified users, secure payments, and delivery codes ensure every transaction is safe and reliable.

Affordability

Fair pricing set by the community. No hidden fees, no surprises - just honest, transparent costs.

Reliability

Real-time tracking, direct communication, and a reputation system you can count on.
