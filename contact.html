Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Is Moova safe to use?

Yes! We use secure escrow payments, verification codes for delivery confirmation, and a rating system to ensure trust between senders and travelers.

What can I send through Moova?

You can send most personal items and gifts. Prohibited items include flammable materials, drugs, loose lithium batteries, cash, and anything illegal.

How do I get paid as a traveler?

You receive 40% of your earnings after package pickup and the remaining 60% once delivery is confirmed with the verification code. Payments go to your Zelle or CashApp.

What happens if my package is lost or damaged?

Contact us immediately through the app or email. We'll work with both parties to resolve the issue. Your payment remains in escrow until delivery is confirmed.

Is the app available in my country?

Moova covers routes between the USA (20+ states) and 54 African countries. Download the app to see available routes for your destination.

How do I become a verified traveler?

Create an account, set up your payment information, and start posting travel offers. Build your reputation by completing deliveries successfully and earning positive reviews.
